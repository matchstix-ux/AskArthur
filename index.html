Meet Arthur, Your AI Cigar Sommelier

Arthur is the smartest guy in the humidor. Tell him what you like, and he'll pull three cigars from the Famous Smoke Shop collection that fit your taste.

🔖

Know what you like?

Give Arthur a brand or a cigar you already enjoy, and he'll find you something in the same neighborhood — or stretch your palate with something new.

🎯

Not sure where to start?

Describe a flavor profile. Creamy and smooth. Spicy with some kick. Earthy and full-bodied. Arthur speaks the language and knows what to reach for.

🥃

Pairing something?

Tell him what you're drinking or eating. Bourbon, espresso, a steak dinner — Arthur will match a cigar to the occasion.

💰

Shopping with a budget?

Whether you want an everyday smoke under ten bucks or a premium stick worth the splurge, Arthur stays in your price range.

Every recommendation comes with tasting notes, strength, price, and a quick explanation of why that cigar fits what you asked for. Like what you see? Hit Buy and you're on the Famous Smoke Shop product page. Want a different option? Hit Replace and Arthur will swap in a fresh pick.

Arthur remembers what you like. When you heart a cigar, he keeps it in mind for next time — so your recommendations get sharper the more you use him.

No account needed. No quiz to fill out. Just tell Arthur what you're in the mood for, and let him do the digging.
